# Welcome to SHADOWCRYSTAL ENGINEERING

### FRC TEAM 11755 | MARYLAND | Montgomery and Fredrick counties
A student-led FIRST robotics team and non-profit STEM organization based in Germantown, Maryland. Our CASTLE TOWN (home base) is located at Seneca Valley High School at Crystal Rock Drive in Germantown.
>no prior experience required
---
## TEAM OVERVIEW
- 4 student-led subteams: Mechanical, Electrical, Software, and Business.
- 11 student members and counting.
- 3 student mentors.
- 5 high schools globally recruited with student impact.
- Completely free: NO prior experience required and finanical contributions required.
---
## WHO WE SERVE
Open to any high school student (grade 9-12) from any school regardless of experience, background, or finanical status.

### ANY SCHOOL
For ALL and EVERY school including Urbana, Damascus, Clarksburg, Richard Montgomery, Seneca Valley High School. Anyone in Montgomery/Frederick counties may attend.

### NO PRIOR EXPERIENCE REQUIRED
Everyone starts somewhere. Our mentors and student leaders provide guidance and learning.
